About Us

Who We Are ?

In today's world, waste management has become a critical challenge. Improper disposal of waste not only harms the environment but also reduces the efficiency of recycling systems. Our mission is to bridge this gap by leveraging cutting-edge technology to create an intelligent waste classification system.

We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated to building a smarter, AI-powered waste classification solution that helps users easily differentiate between recyclable and non-recyclable materials. Using machine learning and automation, our system aims to reduce human error in waste sorting, improve recycling rates, and contribute to a cleaner and greener planet.

By providing an intuitive web-based platform, we empower users to upload images of waste items, which are then classified into various categories such as metal, glass, plastic, and e-waste. Our goal is to streamline waste segregation at the source, making it easier for individuals, businesses, and municipalities to adopt sustainable waste management practices.
